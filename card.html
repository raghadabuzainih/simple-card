Asian elephant

A layer of dense, wrinkled skin on the elephant's body helps regulate its temperature and provides protection against environmental hazards.

  • Scientific Name: Elephas maximus
  • Average Length: 5.5-6.5 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 48 years
  • Habitat: Wet forest and grassland

One of the most majestic and iconic species on Earth. As the largest land animal in Asia and the second-largest in the world after the African elephant, it commands attention with its imposing presence and gentle demeanor.
